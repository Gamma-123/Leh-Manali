Adventure Itinerary

Trip Schedule Trip Inclusions Trip Exclusions Cancellation FAQs
Day 1
-

Arrive in Manali

We Arrive Manali In Morning, Check In Hotel And We Relax Today
To Let Our Body Acclimatize. Explore Manali Local Sight Seeing.
Our Tour Captain Will Give You A Little Brief About The Ride,
Itinerary, Bike Allocation Etc. Evening At Leisure. Explore &
Enjoy Overnight Stay In Hotel With Dinner.

Day 2
-

Manali to Jispa

Your Long-Awaited Ride To Ladakh Finally Begins. Pack Your Bags
And Get Set For A Thrilling Ride. On The Way, You Will Pass Atal
Tunnel, Tandi (Last Petrol Pump Before Upshi) And Keylong.
Once You Reach Jipsa, An Overnight Stay Will Be Provided at camps.

Day 3
-

Jispa to Padum via Shango La Pass

After breakfast, you start the tour towards Zanskar on the just newly
opened road. It’s a bit hard but with the best landscape. It takes
time to cross Shingo la pass and pictures with grand mountain
Gumbo Ranjan(Mahakal) and river crossing, etc. After a meal
from the campsite, you ride to Padum. Tonight you stay at a nice
Home stay or Tents.

Day 4
-

Padum – Kargil

This is a bit of a hard day. This route was just opened in August
2020 that connect you to Leh –Srinagar highway with off road
short cut. Around 240 kilometers’ ride will be covered in daylight.
At night, stay at Hotels.

Day 5
-

Kargil to Leh

This is a bit of a hard day. This route was just opened in August
short cut. Around 240 kilometers’ ride will be covered in daylight.
At night, stay at Hotels.

Day 6
-

Leh to Nubra Valley

This Section Of The Srinagar To Manali Motorbike Expedition
Involves Undertaking A Scenic And Thrilling Road Journey
To The Nubra Valley. The Road To The Valley Will Take
You Across What Is Claimed To Be The Highest Motorable
Road On Earth– Khardung La. Upon Reaching The Nubra
Valley Which Is Located On The BanksOf The Nubra
River, Head To The Hotel For A Relaxing Overnight Stay.

Day 7
-

Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Today Morning, You Will Leave Behind The Stark Region And
Move Toward The Pristine Pangong Lake. En route You Will Pass
Through Shyok Village, Which Has Been Recently Open For The Tourists.
On Reaching The Shores Of This Enchanting Water-body,
Take In Its Beauty And Tranquility, And Capture Photos Of
The Realm. Later, Transfer To The Campsite For A Warming Bonfire
And A Peaceful Overnight Stay In The Lap Of Nature.

Day 8
-

Pangong to Leh

Open your eyes to the scenic view of the Pangong Tso. After
Having Breakfast We Will Leave For Leh. Today We Will Cross
The Second Highest Pass Of The World, Chang La (17,590 ft).
This Pass Is Considered Amongst The Toughest Passes. This
Pass Connects Leh With The Changthang Plateau. Before
Climbing The Mighty Pass We Will See A Small Lake, Known
As Tsoltak. After Crossing The Pass We Will Descend Down
To Shakti And Will Continue The Remaining Journey On The
Manali-Leh Highway. We will Reach Leh By Early Evening
And Enjoy The City And Spend The Night Over There.
